The Legend of Hampas Pinas

Hampas Pinas is the Philippines’ premier independent volleyball hub, built from a deep passion for the sport and a commitment to credible, data-driven coverage.

Created for fans, athletes, and stakeholders alike, Hampas Pinas exists to bridge the gap between the game on the court and the community that lives and breathes it. We provide comprehensive coverage across all levels—from grassroots and collegiate competitions to professional leagues and national team campaigns.

At our core, we value accuracy, context, and storytelling. By combining analytics with narrative, we help fans better understand the game while giving athletes the recognition they’ve earned. Through scouting reports and rankings, we present volleyball as a growing movement.

More than a media platform, Hampas Pinas is a community where Filipino volleyball is celebrated on a global stage.

Mission

To deliver professional-grade volleyball coverage, analytics, and storytelling that elevates the sport across all levels.

Vision

To become the leading digital destination and trusted reference point for the global Filipino volleyball community.

Goal

To amplify local talent, foster engaged fandom, and contribute to the long-term growth of Philippine volleyball.
